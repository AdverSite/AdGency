About

Our humble beginnings.

  • August 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Initial conceptualization of our project. Took a literature survey and understood the concept of generating advertisements.

  • November 2019

    Adgency is born

    The deep learning model was trained so that your advertisments can be identified. the identified images where then processed so that advertisements can be generated.

  • February 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    After building the websites and training the model, our website was deployed in a local host so that it can be used for testing.

  • March 2020

    Further developments

    our further developments mainly included creating an App so that all of this can be done in your hands itself.
